Item 5.07 Submission of Matters to a Vote of Security Holders.

 

On May 29, 2020, Mudrick Capital Acquisition Corporation, a
Delaware corporation (the “Company”) held a special meeting of its stockholders (the “Special Meeting”).
At the Special Meeting, holders of an aggregate of 11,376,591 shares of Class A common stock of the Company, par value $0.0001
per share, and shares of Class B common stock, par value $0.0001 per share (together, the “common stock”), which
represents 93.95% of the shares of common stock outstanding and entitled to vote as of the record date of April 17, 2020, were
represented in person or by proxy.

 

At the Special Meeting, the following proposals were submitted
to and approved by the Company’s stockholders:
