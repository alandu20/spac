Item 5.07 Submission of Matters to a Vote of Security Holders.

 

On January 21, 2020, Alberton Acquisition Corporation
(the “Company”) held its 2019 annual meeting of shareholders (the “Annual Meeting”).
At the Annual Meeting, the Company’s shareholders voted on three proposals, each of which is described in more details in
the Company’s definitive proxy statement filed with the Securities and Exchange Commission on November 18, 2019 (the “Proxy
Statement”). At the beginning of the Annual Meeting, there were 10,204,538 shares of ordinary shares in person or
by proxy, which represented 69.46% of the voting power of the shares of ordinary shares entitled to vote at the Annual Meeting,
which constituted a quorum for the transaction of business.
