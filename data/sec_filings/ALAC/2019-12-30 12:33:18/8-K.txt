Item
8.01 Other Events 

 

On
December 30, 2019, Alberton Acquisition Corporation issued a press release announcing the postponement of its 2019 annual meeting
of shareholders (the “Annual Meeting”) after becoming aware of an error during the mailing process that caused a failure
to timely deliver the proxy materials to beneficial unit holders before the scheduled meeting date. The Annual Meeting was rescheduled
to be held on January 21, 2020 at 10:00 a.m., local time, at Hunter Taubman Fischer & Li LLC, located at 1450 Broadway, 26th
Floor, New York, New York, 10018. A copy of the press release issued by Alberton is attached hereto as Exhibit 99.1 and is incorporated
by reference into this Item 8.01.
