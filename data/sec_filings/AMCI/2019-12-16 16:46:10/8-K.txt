                Item 5.07.
                
                  Submission of Matters to a Vote of Security Holders.
                
              

          
        
      
      
         
        
      
      
        On December 16, 2019, AMCI Acquisition Corp. (the “Company”) held its 2019 annual meeting of stockholders (the “Meeting”).

          At the Meeting, stockholders (i) re-elected one director to serve as the Class I director on the Company’s board of directors (“Board”) until the 2022 annual meeting of stockholders or until his successor
          is elected and qualified and (ii) ratified the selection by the audit committee of the Board of Marcum LLP (“Marcum”) to serve as the Company’s independent registered public accounting firm for the year
          ending December 31, 2019.
