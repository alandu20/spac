Item 8.01. Other Events.

 

Separate Trading of Ordinary Shares, Warrants and Rights

 

On March 13, 2020, East
Stone Acquisition Corporation (the “Company”) announced that the holders of the Company’s units (the “Units”)
may elect to separately trade the securities underlying such Units commencing on March 17, 2020. Each Unit consists of one ordinary
share, no par value (“Ordinary Share”), one warrant to purchase one-half (1/2) of one Ordinary Share and one right
to receive one-tenth (1/10) of one Ordinary Share at the closing of the Company’s initial business combination. Any Units
not separated will continue to trade on the Nasdaq Capital Market under the symbol “ESSCU.” Any underlying Ordinary
Shares, warrants and rights that are separated are expected to trade on the Nasdaq Capital Market under the symbols “ESSC,”
“ESSCW” and “ESSCR,” respectively. Holders of Units will need to have their brokers contact Continental
Stock Transfer & Trust Company, the Company’s transfer agent, in order to separate the holders’ Units into Ordinary
Shares, warrants and rights.

 

A copy of the Press Release
issued by the Company announcing the separate trading of the securities underlying the Units is attached hereto as Exhibit 99.1.
