Item 1.01.Entry into a Material Definitive Agreement.

 

On April 30, 2020 the
Registration Statement on Form S-1 (SEC File No. 333-236798) (the “Registration Statement”) relating to the
initial public offering of units of Collective Growth Corporation (the “Company”) was declared effective by
the Securities and Exchange Commission (“SEC”).

 

On April 30, 2020,
the Company entered into various agreements filed as exhibits to the Registration Statement. The Company also filed an amended
and restated certificate of incorporation with the Secretary of State of the State of Delaware. The material terms of such agreements
and the amended and restated certificate of incorporation are fully described in the Company’s final prospectus, dated April
30, 2020 as filed with the SEC on May 1, 2020. This Current Report on Form 8-K is being filed solely to file such executed agreements
and amended and restated certificate of incorporation.
