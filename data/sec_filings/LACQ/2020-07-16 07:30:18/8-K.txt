Item 1.02Termination of a Material Definitive Agreement.

 

On
July 16, 2020, Leisure Acquisition Corp. (the “Company”) elected to terminate the Agreement and Plan of Merger,
dated December 27, 2019 (the “Merger Agreement”), with GTWY Holdings Limited, a Canadian corporation, and a
related subsidiary, GTWY Merger Sub Corp. Pursuant to its terms, the Company had the ability to terminate the Merger Agreement
to the extent the business combination had not been completed by July 15, 2020.

 

As previously disclosed, the Company’s
stockholders approved an extension to the date by which the Company must complete a business combination to December 1, 2020.
