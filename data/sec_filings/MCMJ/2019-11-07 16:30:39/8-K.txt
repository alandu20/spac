Item
1.01. Entry into a Material Definitive Agreement.

 

On November 4, 2019,
the Registration Statement on Form S-1 (SEC File No. 333-234499) (the “Registration Statement”) relating to
the initial public offering of units of Merida Merger Corp. I (the “Company”) was declared effective by the
Securities and Exchange Commission (“SEC”).

 

On November 4, 2019,
the Company entered into an Underwriting Agreement and various other agreements filed as exhibits to the Registration Statement.
The Company also filed an amended and restated certificate of incorporation with the Secretary of the State of Delaware. The material
terms of such agreements and the amended and restated certificate of incorporation are fully described in the Company’s final
prospectus, dated November 4, 2019 as filed with the SEC on November 5, 2019. This Current Report on Form 8-K is being filed solely
to file such executed agreements and amended and restated certificate of incorporation.
