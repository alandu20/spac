Item 8.01. Other Events.


 

On
March 4, 2019, Andina Acquisition Corp. III (the “Company”) announced that holders of the Company’s units will
be able to separately trade the ordinary shares, rights and warrants included in such units commencing on or about March 6, 2019.
The ordinary shares, rights and warrants will be listed on the Nasdaq Capital Markets under the symbols “ANDA,” “ANDAR”
and “ANDAW,” respectively. Units not separated will continue to be listed on the Nasdaq Capital Markets under the
symbol “ANDAU.” A copy of the Company’s press release announcing the foregoing is included as Exhibit 99.1 to
this Current Report on Form 8-K.
