Item
                                         1.01.Entry
                                         into a Material Definitive Agreement.

 

On
November 6, 2019, the Registration Statement on Form S-1 (SEC File No. 333-234124) (the “Registration Statement”)
relating to the initial public offering of units of LGL Systems Acquisition Corp. (the “Company”) was declared
effective by the Securities and Exchange Commission (“SEC”).

 

On
November 6, 2019, the Company entered into an Underwriting Agreement and various other agreements filed as exhibits to the Registration
Statement. The Company also filed an amended and restated certificate of incorporation with the Secretary of the State of Delaware.
The material terms of such agreements and the amended and restated certificate of incorporation are fully described in the Company’s
final prospectus, dated November 6, 2019 as filed with the SEC on November 8, 2019. This Current Report on Form 8-K is
being filed solely to file such executed agreements and amended and restated certificate of incorporation.
