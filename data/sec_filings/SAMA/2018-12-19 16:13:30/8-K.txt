Item
8.01. Other Events.

 

On
December 13, 2018, Schultze Special Purpose Acquisition Corp. (the “Company”) completed its initial public offering
(“IPO”) of 13,000,000 units (“Units”), each Unit consisting of one share of common stock, par value $0.0001
per share (“Common Stock”) and one warrant (“Warrants”), each Warrant exercisable to purchase one share
of Common Stock, pursuant to the Company’s registration statement on Form S-1, as amended (File No. 333-228494). The Units
were sold at an offering price of $10.00 per Unit, generating gross proceeds of $130,000,000.

 

As
previously reported on a Current Report on Form 8-K of the Company, on December 13, 2018, simultaneously with the consummation
of the IPO, the Company completed a private placement (the “Private Placement”) of an aggregate of 4,150,000 warrants
(the “Private Placement Warrants”) at a price of $1.00 per Private Placement Warrant, generating total proceeds of
$4,150,000. 

 

A
total of $130,000,000 of the net proceeds from the IPO and the Private Placement were deposited in a trust account established
for the benefit of the Company’s public stockholders. An audited balance sheet as of December 13, 2018 reflecting receipt
of the proceeds upon consummation of the IPO and the Private Placement has been issued by the Company and is attached hereto as
Exhibit 99.1.
