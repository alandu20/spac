Item
8.01. Other Events.

 

On June 9, 2020, Novus Capital Corporation. (the “Company”)
announced that holders of the Company’s units will be able to separately trade the shares of common stock and warrants included
in such units commencing on or about June 12, 2020. The common stock and warrants will be listed on the Nasdaq Capital Market under
the symbols “NOVS” and NOVSW” respectively. Units not separated will continue to be listed on the Nasdaq Capital
Market under the symbol “NOVSU.” A copy of the Company’s press release announcing the foregoing is included as
Exhibit 99.1 to this Current Report on Form 8-K.
