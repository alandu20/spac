Item 8.01. Other Events

On April 29, 2020, Newborn
Acquisition Corp. (the “Company”) announced that holders of the Company's units may elect to separately trade the
ordinary shares, warrants and rights included in its units commencing on or about April 30, 2020.

The ordinary shares, warrants and rights will trade on the NASDAQ
Capital Market ("NASDAQ") under the symbols NBAC, NBACW and NBACR, respectively. Units not separated will continue to
trade on NASDAQ under the symbol NBACU. After separation, the ordinary shares, warrants and rights may be recombined to create
units.
