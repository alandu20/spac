Introductory Note

On April 13, 2020,
American Virtual Cloud Technologies, Inc. (f/k/a Pensare Acquisition Corp.), a Delaware corporation (the “Company”),
filed a Current Report on Form 8-K (the “Original Report”) to report the closing of the Merger and related matters
under Items 1.01, 2.01, 2.03, 3.02, 3.03, 5.01, 5.02 and 9.01 of Form 8-K. Due to the large number of events to be reported under
the specified items of Form 8-K, a Form 8-K/A was filed to amend the Original Report to include additional matters related to the
closing of Merger under Items 3.01 and 5.06 of Form 8-K. This Form 8-K/A is being filed to disclose the resignation of Dr. Robert
Willis from his position as President of the Company in connection with the closing of the Merger.

Capitalized terms
used herein by not defined herein have the meanings given to such terms in the Original Report.

Item 5.02. Departure
of Directors or Certain Officers; Election of Directors; Appointment of Certain Officers; Compensatory Arrangements of Certain
Officers. 

Effective
April 7, 2020, Dr. Robert Willis resigned from his position as President of the Company. Following his resignation, Dr. Willis
will continue to provide consulting services to the Company.
