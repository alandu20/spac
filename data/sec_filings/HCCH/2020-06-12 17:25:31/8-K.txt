Item 7.01 Regulation FD Disclosure.

As previously disclosed, on June 6, 2020, HL Acquisitions Corp., a British Virgin Islands business company (“HL”)
entered into a Business Combination Agreement with Fusion Welcome – Fuel, S.A., a public limited company domiciled in Portugal, sociedade
anónima (the “Company”), the shareholders of the Company, and others, pursuant to which HL and the
Company will each become wholly-owned subsidiaries of a newly formed parent entity. The material terms of the business combination
are described in HL’s Current Report on Form 8-K which was filed with the Securities and Exchange Commission on June 8,
2020, and the first amendment to such Current Report, filed on June 9, 2020.

On June 9, 2020, a representative of HL responded in writing to investor questions relating to the previously disclosed business
combination with the Company. Solely to satisfy the requirements of Regulation FD in light of this disclosure, HL is furnishing
as Exhibit 99.1 hereto certain information in question and answer format. The information contained in this Current Report on
Form 8-K, including the related exhibit, supersedes the information disclosed in writing to the investor and, to the extent there
is a conflict between the information contained herein and the prior written information, you should rely on the information contained
herein.

The information furnished hereunder, including the related exhibit, shall not be deemed “filed” for purposes of Section
18 of the Securities Exchange Act of 1934, nor shall it be deemed incorporated by reference in any disclosure document of HL,
except as shall be expressly set forth by specific reference in such document.
