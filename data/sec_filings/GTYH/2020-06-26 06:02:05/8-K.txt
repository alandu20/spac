    Item 5.07.
    Submission of Matters to a Vote of Security Holders.
     

 

On June 23, 2020, GTY
Technology Holdings Inc. (the “Company”) held its annual meeting of the Company’s shareholders (the “annual
meeting”). At the annual meeting, a total of 31,061,552 (57.95%) of the Company’s issued and outstanding shares of
common stock held of record as of April 27, 2020, the record date for the annual meeting, were present either in person or by proxy,
which constituted a quorum. The Company’s shareholders voted on the following proposals at the annual meeting, each of which
were approved. The final vote tabulation for each proposal is set forth below.
